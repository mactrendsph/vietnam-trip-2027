🗺️ About the Destinations

🏙️ Đà Nẵng

  • Vietnam's 3rd largest city, on the central coast
  • Famous for Mỹ Khê Beach (My Khe), Dragon Bridge
  • Gateway to Bà Nà Hills & Golden Bridge
  • ~1.5 hrs from Hội An by road (32 km)
  • Airport: Đà Nẵng International (DAD)

🏮 Hội An

  • UNESCO World Heritage Ancient Town
  • Famous for lanterns, tailors, cuisine & Thu Bồn River
  • Best explored on foot or bicycle
  • ~30 min drive from Đà Nẵng
  • Best: sunset at the old town & river cruise

💡 Flight Tips

📌 Booking Tips

  • Book at least 3–6 months in advance for group of 10–20
  • Consider group booking discounts — call airlines directly
  • MNL → DAD direct: ~3 hours · Via SGN/HAN: ~5–7 hours
  • Cebu Pacific & AirAsia often have promos — check their sale calendars
  • Baggage allowance: agree on a standard (20–25kg checked recommended)
  • Travel insurance is highly recommended for the whole group

🧳 Packing Checklist
🛂 Travel Requirements (PH → VN)

🇵🇭 Philippine Citizens

  • Visa-free entry to Vietnam for up to 30 days
  • Valid passport (min. 6 months from travel date)
  • Return/onward ticket required
  • No visa needed — just show at immigration
  • E-visa available if staying longer than 30 days

📋 Immigration Tips

  • Fill out arrival card on the plane
  • Keep hotel address handy (immigration may ask)
  • Currency declaration not required under $5,000 USD
  • Health declaration: check current requirements

📱 Useful Apps & Tips

📱 Essential Apps

  • Grab — rides & food delivery
  • Google Maps (download offline)
  • Google Translate (VN offline pack)
  • XE Currency — real-time exchange
  • Klook / Viator — activities booking

📡 Connectivity

  • SIM card at airport (~100k–150k VND)
  • Recommended: Viettel or Vietnamobile
  • 3GB–5GB data for ~7 days
  • Free WiFi at most hotels & restaurants
  • E-SIM: Airalo works well in Vietnam

💡 Money Tips

  • ATMs widely available in Da Nang
  • Notify your bank before travel
  • Cash is king in local markets/Hoi An
  • Exchange at banks for better rates
  • Avoid airport money changers (bad rates)
